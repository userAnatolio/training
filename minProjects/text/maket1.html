Restoration of cars

"Retro-Motors" is a company specializing in services related to retro cars: sale, investment and creation of collections, complete restoration or partial (restoration of the car or body salon), maintenance, conservation and consultation. Retro cars, produced more than 30 years ago, in many countries are called old timers. And after all, really, looking at them it seems as if he got into another time. But the time about which cars can tell a lot was inexorable to them, in places really mercilessly: they grew old and rusted, sound and slowly, year by year, structural chemical changes occurred in the materials from which the parts were made, and the structural elements accumulated microdamages caused by exploitation. Restoration of the car is a painstaking and lengthy process. To complete with original parts and restore the collection car can take years, but in each case there are nuances.

Dismantling and dismantling

Any restoration of cars begins with the dismantling of the cabin, body, running gear, units and assemblies. The machine is untwisted, as it is called before the bolt, this is the only way to evaluate the degree of wear and determine which parts are original and which parts have changed. Our main capital is books and technical documentation; in the process of work, the existing equipment is checked against the literature on this model (drawings, spare parts catalogs, repair manuals, etc.).

Production

Whether it's a vintage 1-cylinder Cadillac in 1908, or a Soviet representative ZIS 110, every detail is compared to a spare parts catalog. After this, a list of original replacement parts or lost parts is created, which must be completed by the machine.

Welding

Welding of ferrous metals is carried out according to generally accepted norms and technologies. We use welding equipment for arc welding with coated electrodes, semi-automatic welding with a consumable electrode in carbon dioxide and gas welding.
